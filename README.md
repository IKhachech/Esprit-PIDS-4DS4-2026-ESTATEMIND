# Esprit-PIDS-4DS4-2026-ESTATEMIND

## Overview
Estate Mind is an AI-powered real estate analytics platform designed to provide property valuation, market trend prediction, legal risk analysis, and personalized investment decision support.

Developed at Esprit School of Engineering – Tunisia.  
PIDEV – 4th Year Engineering Program (Academic Year 2025–2026).

## Features
- Property valuation using Machine Learning
- Market trend prediction (spatio-temporal analysis)
- Legal risk analysis of real estate contracts
- Investment scenario generation
- Multi-source real estate data integration
